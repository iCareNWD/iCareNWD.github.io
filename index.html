Frequently Asked Questions




	
    
    
		
		
  • 
			
    
				Who can use iCare?
			
    
			
    
				iCare App will be available for FREE on different OS. (e.g. Google Play Store & IOS App Store). 
    

        You can use it to monitor the health status of yourself, or your beloved parents.
			
    
		
    • 
		
  • 
			
    
				What if my mum/dad doesn’t use smartphone?
			
    
			
    
				No worries! Your parents just need a smartwatch. Application can be downloaded by you to monitor their health status.
			
    
		
    • 
		
  • 
			
    
				What devices can the app be applied to?
			
    
			
    
				Our App and services can be applied to smartwatches, smartphones and any form of IoT devices (e.g. Electronic Blood Pressure Meter or Blood Sugar Meter).
			
    
		
    • 
		
  • 
			
    
				Is the analysis accurate?
			
    
			
    
				Profile will be generated according to users’ data. Machine learning, clustering and classification techniques will be used for further training for the model. The longer you use the application, more data could be collected and analysed. You could also input your medical history to have a more precise result. Environmental factors, such as temperature and humidity, would be also taken into account. Seasonality will also be take into account. For example, cardiovascular disease would be easier to be triggered in winter which is proved by various academic study worldwide.
